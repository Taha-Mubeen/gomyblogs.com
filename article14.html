Apple IPhone 13 Series

Apple Inc is an American multinational technology company that provides its users latest electronic equipment with its all-new latest technology. Apple provides the fastest technology with its best peak performance. It gives the most adorable features in its devices providing the best security of data to its users. Apple uses its own IOS software in its devices which is well known for its unbeatable performance. Apple was founded on April 1, 1976, in California, United States. Its founder was Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. Since August 24, 2011, Apple’s CEO is Tim Cook. Apple’s net worth in 2022 is $2850 Billion ($2.850 trillion).

Every year from October to November at the Apple WWDC event, Apple launches its new iPhone series. On September 14, 2021, Apple launches its iPhone 13 series. It comes in four models:

  • iPhone 13
  • iPhone 13 mini
  • iPhone 13 pro
  • iPhone 13 pro max

iPhone 13:

  • Apple announced iPhone 13 on September 14, 2021, and release it on September 24, 2021. Its dimension is 146.7 x 71.5 x 7.7 mm (5.78 x 2.81 x 0.30 in) and 174g weight. Its size is 6.1 inches.
  • iPhone 13 front and back are made up of gorilla glass and it has an aluminum frame. It comes in a single sim (Nano-SIM and/or ESIM) and dual sim (Nano-SIM, dual stand-by).
  • iPhone 13 back glass has shiny glossy finishing.
  • iPhone 13 supports its feature of dust and water resistance. It is water resistance up to 6m for 30min.
  • It provides a super retina XDR OLED display with a resolution of 1170 x 2532 pixels, 19.5:9 ratio, and its display are protected with ceramic scratch resistance glass with oleophobic coating. Its display supports the true tone feature.
  • iPhone 13 was launched with IOS 15 which is till April 2022 upgraded to IOS 15.4.1.
  • iPhone 13 comes with most fastest Apple A15 bionic chip which is 5nm small and has Apple GPU (4-core graphics).
  • iPhone 13 comes with 128, 256, 512 GB storage space and 4 GB RAM.
  • iPhone 13 has two back cameras. One is 12 MP, f/1.6, 26mm (wide), 1.7µm, dual pixel PDAF, sensor-shift OIS, and the other is 12 MP, f/2.4, 120˚, 13mm (ultrawide).
  • Its front camera is 12 MP, f/2.2, 23mm (wide), 1/3.6" and its basic features are face ID, accelerometer, gyro, proximity, compass, and barometer.
  • iPhone 13 has a Li-Ion battery of 3240 mAh which is non-removable and it also provides fast charging of 23W which charge mobile 50% in 30mins.
  • iPhone 13 comes in Starlight, Midnight, Blue, Pink, Red, and Green colors.
  • iPhone 13 starts from $799.
