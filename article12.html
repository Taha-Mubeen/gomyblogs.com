Most Used Social Media Platforms around the Globe

Social media is a digital platform used for social interaction between its users. It is also used to gain information. It is an internet-based form of communication. Internet is a major requirement for a user to get benefit from it. It provides an opportunity to share information between users. The majority of social media apps help us to communicate with each having long or short distances between each other. We can share our content, can watch each other, and also can arrange a meeting or conference between people using social apps. We can have an eye on the whole world with these apps and we can get the latest news in instant time.

There are a lot of apps having a different wide range of benefits respectively. Some apps perform the function of communication some provides information platform, entertainment platform, news platforms, etc.

We can have a look at the three most used worldwide social apps for their most famous functionality.

Facebook:

  • Facebook, inc. is a META technology app. The founder of Facebook Mark Zuckerberg introduces Facebook in February 2004.
  • Facebook is functional under META technology company which is an American multinational technology conglomerate based in Menlo Park, California. It is the parent organization of Facebook, WhatsApp, Twitter, Instagram, and many other subsidiaries.
  • Facebook is the most popular social media worldwide. As of the third quarter of 2021, there were around 3 billion to 5 billion cumulative monthly users of the company's products worldwide of which 2.9 billion monthly active users.
  • According to of 19 April 2022 Facebook’s stock price is US$216.35. statics of January 2022 India is no country with 329.6 million users. The US is the second number with 179.65 million users and Indonesia takes its position third with 129.85 million users.
  • Facebook offers a lot of features to its new users. One most basic feature is its purpose of communication. Using Facebook we can communicate with each other and also can make new friends from any corner of the world. We can post videos photos on our timeline and it allows our friends to like share and comment on our posts. We can also create groups, pages, and communities on Facebook. Using Facebook messenger we can message, call, and can make video calls. Facebook is providing a feature to make your community and you can add your friends to it. We can share photos, videos, etc.

YouTube:

  • YouTube is an American company. It is an online video-sharing social media platform in which one can upload videos or movies and all users on YouTube can watch it.
  • Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karim launched YouTube on February 14, 2005. Since February 5, 2014, Susan Wojcicki is the CEO of YouTube. YouTube headquarters are located in San Bruno, California, United States. Its parent organization is GOOGLE.
  • YouTube is owned by Alphabet Inc. According to 2021 YouTube’s revenue is 28.8 billion USD. On April 19, 2022, in stock YouTube share is at $2,597.26
  • YouTube users are approximately 2,240.03 million in the world. In 2025, the expected number of YouTube users in the world will reach 2,854.14 million.
  • YouTube is used for entertainment purposes. Everyone can easily upload their content, videos, movies, etc. Same as everyone on YouTube can watch uploaded content. You can also get income through YouTube depending on your content viewers. YouTube also implies a few restrictions like you cannot upload nudity content if it is not informative. You can also apply age restrictions to your videos.

WhatsApp:

  • WhatsApp, is a mobile application that is internationally available American freeware, cross-platform centralized instant messaging and voice-over-IP service which is owned by Meta Platforms.
  • WhatsApp can also operate on laptops and computers using WhatsApp Web. WhatsApp is a communication app used to communicate having large distances free of cost.
  • WhatsApp was founded by Brian Acton and Jan Koum. They were former employees of Yahoo. At the start, WhatsApp was not intended to be a messaging app. Later it turned into a messaging app. WhatsApp was initially released in January 2009.
  • According to statics for 2021 WhatsApp has more than 2 billion users. 100 billion messages are exchanged on WhatsApp daily. Its 22% of users are IOS and 73% users are Android. WhatsApp is the third most used social app.
  • WhatsApp is a communication app. It allows its users to message each other, and share pics, and videos. We can send and receive voice messages, calls, and video calls. You can also upload one-day status. WhatsApp is a free communication app that makes itself more attractive to its users.

Instagram:

  • Instagram is a social networking app. It is an American video and photo sharing social networking service app. Instagram was launched on October 6, 2010. Its founder was Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger.
  • In April 2012 Instagram’s ownership changed, Facebook acquired it for US 1 billion dollars approximately in cash and stocks. Currently, Instagram developers are meta platforms. In 2012 Instagram won a shorty award for apps for their excellent performance.
  • On IOS Instagram take 197.5 MB of space while on android Instagram take 50.19 MB space and 43.5 MB space on Fire OS. Instagram is also nominated for the teen choice award for the chosen social network.
  • Instagram 31% users are 18 to 24 years old. Of 31% of users, 17.9% of users are male while 13.1% of users are female. There are more than 2 billion monthly active users of Instagram and 1.9 billion daily active users. There are more than 200 million businesses on Instagram. In 2021 there were 1074 million monthly users of Instagram while in 2013 there were only 150 million monthly users.
  • Instagram is a social communication app. It offers its users to message each other and make calls and video calls. Users can upload their pictures and video to their accounts. Their followers can like comments and also have an option to share them with others who have followed them. Instagram also provides the option to its users to upload content to their stories which will appear for the next 2 hours. It also provides options to make your content private or also can block and restrict others. You can also make conversation groups and can add others to these conversation groups. There are two options for an account one is its followers and the other is its following. Followers are those accounts that have followed that user while following is which account the user has followed. There is no restriction to log in to only one account users can log in to multiple accounts in a single app.
