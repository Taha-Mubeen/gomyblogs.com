World’s first iPhone | iPhone 2G

Apple Inc is an American multinational technology company that provides its users latest electronic equipment with its all-new latest technology. Apple provides the fastest technology with its best peak performance. It gives the most adorable features in its devices providing the best security of data to its users. Apple uses its own IOS software in its devices which is well known for its unbeatable performance. Apple was founded on April 1, 1976, in California, United States. Its founder was Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. Since August 24, 2011, Apple’s CEO is Tim Cook. Apple’s net worth in 2022 is $2850 Billion ($2.850 trillion).

Apple’s first iPhone is known as:

  • Apple iPhone 1st generation.
  • Somewhere it is known as iPhone 1.
  • Generally Apple’s first smartphone is known as iPhone 2G.

Apple iPhone 1st generation

  • Apple iPhone 1st generation was announced in January 2007 and it was launched in June 2007. Its current status is discontinued.
  • Apple uses GSM networking technology in its first iPhone.
  • Its dimensions are 115 x 61 x 11.6 mm which in inches is as 4.53 x 2.40 x 0.46 in. Its mass is about 135g.
  • Its sim size is mini sim and its back cover is sealed with screws (non-removable).
  • Apple iPhone 1st generation has a TFT display and its size is 3.5 inches, 36.5 cm2, and its screen to body ratio is ~52.0.
  • It has a resolution of 320 x 480 pixels, 3:2 ratio and it is protected with corning gorilla glass and oleophobic coating.
  • Apple uses IOS software from its 1st generation iPhone which was later upgraded to IOS 3.1.3. Its CPU is 412 MHz ARM 11 and its GPU is PowerVR MBX.
  • Apple doesn’t give any kind of memory card slot from its 1st iPhone, while it comes in three kinds of memory variants 4GB, 8GB, and 16GB internal storage.
  • Its main back camera is 2MP.
  • Apple iPhone 1st generation doesn’t have a video recorder camera and also it doesn’t have any flashlight in it.
  • This iPhone also doesn’t have any kind of front camera.
  • This iPhone has a loudspeaker and its alert types are vibration and proprietary ringtone. Its WLAN is Wi-Fi 802.11b/g and 2.0 Bluetooth which has headset support only.
  • Apple iPhone 1st generation doesn’t have GPS and radio and it has 2.0 USB.
  • Its basic sensors were Accelerometer and proximity.
  • Its browser was HTML (Safari) and also has Google Maps, Audio/Video Player, TV-out, Organizer, Document viewer, Photo viewer, and Predictive text input.
  • Apple iPhone 1st generation has a 1400 mAh non-removable Li-Ion battery.
  • Apple iPhone 1st generation has come only in one color (Black).
  • Its price was about 420 EUR.

Apple iPhone 1st generation was released on June 29, 2007 and it was discontinued on 15 July, 2008. There was 6.1 million units sold. It was launched with IOS 1.0 and its last update was IOS 3.1.3 and its last update was released on February 2, 2010. Its RAM was 128 MB. Comparing 1st iPhone with current iPhone, first iPhone has a home button on it.
